Home energy management system (HEMS) is an effective hardware and software system that facilitate home users to monitor and reduce energy consumption of the various electrical devices installed in their homes. HEMS comprises five different products, which assist in managing and reducing energy consumption. These products include self-monitoring systems, lighting controls, programmable communicating thermostats, advanced central controllers and intelligent HVAC controllers. With HEMS, end-user can access real-time energy consumption data through mobile phones, tablets, and other communication devices to monitor and manage energy consumption in the house. In addition, HEMS provides updates on fluctuating electricity prices to assist users to use less energy during peak hours.
